Liu Yang (1978-) was born in a workers family, and she grew up loving books and cooking. She graduated from the PLA Air Force Aviation University, qualifying as a pilot. She became deputy head of a flight unit, holding the rank of major. She has more than 1600 hours of flying experience, and was selected for the crew of Shenzhou 9, the first manned mission to the Chinese space station Tiangong 1. She excelled during the two years of astronaut training, and during the 13 days of her space mission she performed experiments in space medicine.‚Å†
