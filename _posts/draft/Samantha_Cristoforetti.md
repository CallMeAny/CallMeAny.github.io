Samantha Cristoforetti (1977-) is an Italian astronaut, and she holds the record for the longest uninterrupted European spaceflight (199 days). When she was 18 she took an exchange program to the USA and attended Space Camp, after that she became one of the first women in the Italian air force to be lieutenant and fighter pilot. She is the first person who brewed an espresso in space. In her book she describes her first hand experience as astronaut trainee.â
